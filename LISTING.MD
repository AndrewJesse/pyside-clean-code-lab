HMI Software Architect and Debugger

Inside the Role

We are seeking a software architect and debugger to shape the future Driver Experience domain for Daimler Truck and its brands. 

You will lead the SW architecture development of one of our new Daimler Truck HMI ECUs (Instrument Cluster). In collaboration with our global development team, you will deliver forward-thinking, robust, and maintainable solutions for all Daimler Truck brands. Your involvement will span all development phases, including ideation, architecture, prototyping, validation and release. 

The Driver Experience team focuses on digital on-truck solutions and works hand in hand with design, engineering, and product strategy teams globally.


Job Level: L5  Compensation Grade: CG13

-

What You Drive at DTNA

Lead software architecture and feature development and implementation decisions

Define and govern a scalable SW architecture

Support with pre-analysis of defects: identify root cause and possible fix

For critical defects: ensure quick turnaround by supporting debug data and directly implementing possible fixes in prototype builds

Collaborate with software defined vehicle project team to develop consistent and aligned concepts

Collaborate with a diverse team of designers, UI/UX experts, feature owners, project teams, and developers located in Germany, India, and United States

Coach and mentor team members and colleagues on all aspects of development – from methodologies to execution

Create documentation, reports, presentations
